THE ULTIMATE PROTEIN SHAKE RECIPE

Tons of protein, Tons of easy calories, this bad booy will sure keep you full and prepare you for along day of work and lifting!

Ingredients:

  • 1 scoop of vanilla ice cream
  • 1 large banana
  • 250 ml skimmed milk
  • 25 gm peanut butter
  • a dash of cinnamon

Steps:

  1. add all ingrediets to a blender
  2. blend!
  3. enjoy
