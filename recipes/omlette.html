CHEESY OMLETTE RECIPE

A nice filling protein packed omlette, just like mama used to make!

Ingredients:

  • 4 large eggs
  • 30 gm of shredded cheddar cheese
  • 5 gm of butter
  • 2 slices of whole grain toast
  • lotsa salt and pepper

Steps:

  1. beat your eggs, adding salt and pepper
  2. in a low heat pan, melt the butter, making sure it covers the entire surface of the pan
  3. cook until the eggs form a solid base
  4. add the cheese on top and fold the omlette (you can add any other veggies/meats you want)
  5. remove from pan, toast some toast as a side and Enjoy!
