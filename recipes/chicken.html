CLASSIC CHICKEN BREAST BUT BETTER

Good ol reliable chicken breast, we all love it, but let's spice it up!

Ingredients:

  • 300 gm of raw chicken breast
  • 1 tablespoon mustard
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 3 tespoon garlic powder
  • lotsa salt and pepper

Steps:

  1. cut all your chicken breast into fine -shawerma like- pieces
  2. add all other ingredints on it and let it marinate in the fridge for at least 30 min
  3. add the chicken to a medium high heat pan and kepp it movin, until chicken is brown gold-ish
  4. Enjoy!
